One Piece Game

This game is called One Piece. It is a simple adventure game made with UWP (Universal Windows Platform). The player controls Luffy, a character from the anime One Piece. The goal is to collect crew members like Sanji, Zoro, Nami, and Robin while avoiding obstacles like rocks and waves. The game has three difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, and Hard. Each level changes the number of collectibles, obstacles, and the time limit. The game also includes a score system and saves the top 5 high scores in a file. If the player gets a new high score, they can enter their name.

Contoso Power Bi

This Power BI project analyzes the Contoso Retail Data Warehouse to provide actionable insights for management. The dashboard answers key business questions such as identifying best-selling and underperforming products, determining the most profitable channels, and evaluating profitability trends across years, quarters, and months. The solution includes an optimized data model and interactive visualizations—such as KPIs, bar charts, line charts, and slicers—designed to highlight critical metrics and support data-driven decision-making.
