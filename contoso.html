Contoso Power BI Dashboard

Stack: Power BI, DAX, Data Modeling, Data Visualization

Overview

A complete Power BI solution analyzing Contoso retail performance: KPIs, profitability, top-performing products, and multi-year trends. Built with an optimized star schema and interactive visuals.

Data Model

  • Star schema with fact and dimension tables.
  • Active relationships and time intelligence functions.
  • DAX measures for KPIs and ratios (e.g., Margin %, YoY Sales).
-- Example DAX
Total Sales = SUM(FactSales[SalesAmount])
YoY Sales = CALCULATE([Total Sales], SAMEPERIODLASTYEAR('Date'[Date]))
Margin % = DIVIDE([Total Margin], [Total Sales])

Visuals & Interactions

  • KPI cards, line charts, bar charts, and geographic maps.
  • Bookmarks for switching views (Performance / Profitability).
  • Drill-through to product and store details.

Insights & Impact

  • Identified top-margin products and seasonal patterns.
  • Prioritized stores by performance and profitability.
  • Supported pricing and promotional decisions.
